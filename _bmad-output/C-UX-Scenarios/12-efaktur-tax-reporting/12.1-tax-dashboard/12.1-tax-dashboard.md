## Page Purpose

Tax compliance overview with e-Faktur generation alerts and submission deadlines

---

## Key Components

### 1. e-Faktur Alert (Conditional)
- Display when: Before 15th of next month AND unprocessed invoices exist
- Message: "e-Faktur Due - X invoices need e-Faktur before 15 Mei 2026"
- Deadline countdown: "5 days remaining"
- Action: "Generate e-Faktur →" (primary button)

### 2. Tax Compliance Summary
**Three Cards:**
- **This Month**: PPN Keluaran, PPN Masukan, Net PPN
- **e-Faktur Status**: Generated, Pending, Rejected
- **Submission Status**: On Time, Late, Penalties

### 3. Unprocessed Invoices Widget
- Count: "52 invoices without e-Faktur"
- Total amount: "Rp 850.500.000"
- Action: "Process Now →"

### 4. Recent e-Faktur Activity
- Timeline of recent generations
- XML exports
- DJP submissions

### 5. Quick Actions
- Generate e-Faktur
- Export XML
- SPT Masa PPN
- Tax Reports

---

## Interactions

- "Generate e-Faktur" → Navigate to Sales Invoice List (12.2)
- "Process Now" → Navigate to Sales Invoice List (12.2)
- Alert click → Navigate to Sales Invoice List (12.2)
