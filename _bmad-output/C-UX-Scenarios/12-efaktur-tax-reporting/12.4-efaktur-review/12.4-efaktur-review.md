## Validation Checks

**Critical (must pass):**
- NPWP format valid (15 digits)
- DPP calculation correct
- PPN rate correct (11% or 12%)
- e-Faktur number format valid

**Warning (can proceed):**
- Customer address incomplete
- Phone number missing
- Email missing

---

## Interactions

- Click row → Expand detail view
- Click e-Faktur# → Open full detail modal
- Edit → Open edit form
- Delete → Confirm dialog → Remove e-Faktur
- "Export XML" → Navigate to XML Export (12.5)
