**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
View real-time dashboards → drill-down analytics → make strategic decisions based on current data

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** 🌟 TERTIARY: Business Intelligence (Real-Time Insights)  
**Objective:** Owner sees real-time business health, not delayed monthly reports

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Pak Hendra (Company Owner, TERTIARY)  
**Situation:** Morning, checking business health before meetings. Wants real-time revenue/profit/cash flow data, not wait for monthly close (3 days delay).

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** Real-time business metrics visible anytime, drill-down per warehouse/product/customer, strategic decisions made with confidence.

**Worry:** Delayed reports (wait for monthly close), no visibility into business health, decisions made on outdated data.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Desktop (office), Mobile (on-the-go - future)  
**Entry:** Daily routine — morning dashboard check before meetings, or anytime strategic decision needed.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Real-time business metrics visible (revenue, profit, cash flow), drill-down to warehouse/product level, strategic decisions made with confidence. No waiting for monthly reports.

**Business Success:**
Strategic decisions based on real-time data (not delayed), competitive advantage (faster response to market), owner confidence in business health visibility.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **Main Dashboard (Role-Based)** — Pak Hendra sees owner dashboard: revenue, profit, cash flow (real-time)
2. **Drill-Down Views** — Clicks revenue chart, drills down per warehouse/product/customer
3. **Notification Center** — Sees alerts (low stock, pending approvals, late payments)
4. **Audit Log** — Reviews recent system activity for compliance/security ✓
