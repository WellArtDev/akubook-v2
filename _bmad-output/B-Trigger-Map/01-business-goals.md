# Business Goals & Objectives

> Strategic goals and measurable objectives for AkuBook

**Document:** Trigger Map - Business Goals  
**Created:** 2026-05-12  
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Vision

**Membebaskan bisnis Indonesia dari subscription fatigue dan vendor lock-in dengan memberikan kontrol penuh atas data dan operasi bisnis mereka melalui all-in-one ERP yang terjangkau, modular, dan dapat di-deploy on-premise.**

---

## Business Objectives

### ⭐ PRIMARY GOAL: Reduce Finance Admin Manual Work by 95% (THE ENGINE)

- **Statement:** Finance Admin hanya review & approve transactions, bukan manual entry — 95%+ transactions auto-post to journal
- **Metric:** Percentage of transactions that auto-post to journal without manual intervention
- **Target:** 95%+ auto-posting rate
- **Timeline:** Month 6 post-launch
- **Impact:** Ini adalah THE ENGINE yang menggerakkan semua objectives lain. Ketika Finance Admin terbebas dari manual entry, mereka bisa fokus ke strategic analysis dan business insights. Ini yang membuat AkuBook berbeda dari Accurate — bukan cuma "accounting software", tapi "business intelligence platform" yang kebetulan punya accounting module.

---

### 🚀 OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY (Driven by Auto-Posting Engine)

**Objective 1: Faster Monthly Close**
- **Statement:** Monthly close process dari 3 hari menjadi < 8 jam
- **Metric:** Time to complete monthly close (from last transaction to final reports)
- **Target:** < 8 hours
- **Timeline:** Month 6 post-launch
- **Impact:** Auto-posting engine menghilangkan bottleneck manual entry, sehingga monthly close tinggal review & reconciliation

**Objective 2: Real-Time Inventory Visibility**
- **Statement:** Warehouse staff, Sales, dan Purchasing bisa lihat real-time stock across all warehouses
- **Metric:** Stock data latency (time from transaction to visibility)
- **Target:** < 5 seconds
- **Timeline:** Month 3 post-launch
- **Impact:** Eliminates "call warehouse to check stock" friction — Sales bisa langsung quote, Purchasing bisa langsung reorder

**Objective 3: Automated Payroll Processing**
- **Statement:** HRD Manager process payroll dalam 4 jam (vs 2 hari manual)
- **Metric:** Time to process monthly payroll for 100+ employees
- **Target:** < 4 hours
- **Timeline:** Month 6 post-launch
- **Impact:** Attendance data auto-sync dari ZKTeco/mobile → auto-calculate lembur/cuti → generate payslip

---

### 🌟 BUSINESS CONTROL & OWNERSHIP (Real-World Benefits for Companies)

**Note:** Ini adalah benefits yang AkuBook berikan UNTUK companies — bukan cuma "features", tapi fundamental business advantages yang competitor subscription model tidak bisa kasih.

**Objective 4: Data Sovereignty**
- **Statement:** Companies own their data 100% — on-premise deployment option
- **Metric:** Percentage of customers choosing on-premise vs cloud
- **Target:** 40%+ choose on-premise
- **Timeline:** Month 12 post-launch
- **Benefit to Companies:** No vendor lock-in, no data hostage situation, full control over backup/security/compliance

**Objective 5: Cost Predictability**
- **Statement:** One-time payment eliminates subscription fatigue — 50% cheaper than Accurate lifetime cost
- **Metric:** Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) over 5 years vs Accurate
- **Target:** 50% lower TCO
- **Timeline:** Immediate (pricing model)
- **Benefit to Companies:** Predictable budgeting, no surprise price hikes, no "pay more for more users" trap

**Objective 6: Seamless Migration**
- **Statement:** Companies migrate from Accurate/Excel tanpa buang historical data
- **Metric:** Successful data migration rate (opening balances + historical transactions)
- **Target:** 90%+ migration success rate
- **Timeline:** Month 9 post-launch
- **Benefit to Companies:** No "start from zero" pain, preserve audit trail, maintain business continuity
