## Profile Summary

**Pak Hendra, 45 tahun, Owner PT Distributor Sound System (100+ karyawan, Rp 2M revenue/bulan)**

Pak Hendra adalah company owner yang built his business dari nol 15 tahun lalu. Sekarang company sudah established dengan 3 gudang, 100+ karyawan, dan Rp 2M revenue/bulan. Dia pakai Accurate Online untuk accounting, tapi frustrated dengan subscription costs yang terus naik (Rp 300k/bulan → Rp 400k/bulan), no on-premise option, dan vendor lock-in. Dia ingin control his data, predictable costs, dan freedom to choose deployment model.

**Current Reality:** Subscription fatigue, vendor lock-in, no data control  
**Desired Reality:** One-time payment, on-premise option, full data ownership  
**Transformation:** Subscription prisoner → Business freedom owner

---

## Background

Pak Hendra started PT Distributor Sound System 15 tahun lalu dengan 1 gudang dan 10 karyawan. Grow organically ke 3 gudang dan 100+ karyawan. Revenue sekarang Rp 2M/bulan, profit margin 15-20%.

**Business Journey:**
- Year 1-5: Manual Excel accounting, manual attendance (paper-based)
- Year 6-10: Adopt Accurate Online (Rp 200k/bulan), ZKTeco attendance machines
- Year 11-15: Expand ke 3 gudang, subscription costs naik (Rp 300k → Rp 400k/bulan), frustrated dengan vendor lock-in

**Current Pain:** Subscription costs terus naik, no negotiation power, data hostage situation (can't export full historical data), no on-premise option (forced cloud dependency).

---

## Current Situation

**Pain Points:**
1. **Subscription Fatigue:** Accurate Rp 400k/bulan (naik dari Rp 200k), no end in sight, no negotiation power
2. **Vendor Lock-In:** Can't switch easily — data export limited, migration complex, historical data hostage
3. **No Data Control:** Forced cloud dependency, no on-premise option, data sovereignty concerns
4. **Tool Sprawl:** Accurate (accounting) + Excel (attendance) + Manual surat jalan + WhatsApp (communication) = disconnected
5. **Hidden Costs:** Subscription + training + support + upgrades = unpredictable TCO

**Current Workflow:**
- Monthly: Pay Rp 400k subscription (Accurate) + Rp 100k (other tools) = Rp 500k/bulan = Rp 6 juta/tahun
- Quarterly: Review financial reports dari Finance Admin (3 hari monthly close = delayed insights)
- Annually: Negotiate dengan Accurate (no leverage, price hike inevitable)

**Frustration:** "Saya bayar subscription selamanya, tapi data saya hostage. Kalau saya stop bayar, data saya hilang. Ini bukan ownership, ini rental."

## 6 Driving Forces

### ✅ WANTS (Positive Drivers)

**1. Want: Escape Subscription Fatigue**
- **AkuBook Promise:** One-time payment Rp 25 juta (vs Accurate Rp 400k/bulan × 60 bulan = Rp 24 juta over 5 years) — 50% cheaper TCO
- **How:** Perpetual license model, all modules included, unlimited users, free updates for 1 year
- **Impact:** Predictable budgeting, no surprise price hikes, CFO approval easier

**2. Want: Data Sovereignty**
- **AkuBook Promise:** On-premise deployment option — full data control, no cloud dependency, backup/security your way
- **How:** Self-hosted option (SQLite/PostgreSQL), cloud option (for those who want it), hybrid option (best of both)
- **Impact:** Data sovereignty, compliance confidence, no vendor hostage situation

**3. Want: Business Intelligence**
- **AkuBook Promise:** Real-time dashboards — see business health anytime, not wait for monthly close
- **How:** Role-based dashboards (owner sees revenue/profit/cash flow), drill-down per warehouse/product/customer
- **Impact:** Strategic decisions based on real-time data, not delayed reports

### ❌ FEARS (Negative Drivers)

**4. Fear: Migration Nightmare**
- **AkuBook Answer:** Seamless data import dari Accurate — opening balances, historical transactions, chart of accounts preserved
- **How:** Built-in migration wizard, validation checks, rollback option, parallel run support (run Accurate & AkuBook simultaneously for 1 month)
- **Impact:** No business disruption, audit trail preserved, reversible if needed

**5. Fear: Hidden Costs**
- **AkuBook Answer:** Transparent pricing — Rp 25 juta one-time, no per-user fees, no module fees, no surprise charges
- **How:** All-inclusive pricing, free updates for 1 year, optional support contract (Rp 2 juta/year), no mandatory upgrades
- **Impact:** Predictable TCO, no budget surprises, CFO confidence

**6. Fear: Vendor Dependency**
- **AkuBook Answer:** Open architecture — modular design, API access, data export anytime, no lock-in
- **How:** REST API untuk integrations, CSV/Excel export anytime, database access (on-premise), extensibility via modules
- **Impact:** No vendor lock-in, freedom to integrate/extend, future-proof investment
