## Profile Summary

**Sari, 32 tahun, Finance Admin di PT Distributor Sound System (100+ karyawan, 3 gudang)**

Sari adalah Finance Admin yang handle accounting, AP/AR, monthly close, dan financial reports. Setiap hari dia tenggelam dalam manual data entry — compile data dari Accurate, Excel attendance, dan manual surat jalan. Monthly close butuh 3 hari karena harus reconcile data dari berbagai sources. Dia tahu ada cara lebih baik, tapi stuck dengan tools yang disconnected dan subscription costs yang terus naik.

**Current Reality:** Manual entry 60% of her time, monthly close 3 days, frustrated with tool sprawl  
**Desired Reality:** Review & approve only, monthly close < 8 hours, strategic analysis focus  
**Transformation:** Manual data entry clerk → Strategic financial analyst

---

## Background

Sari graduated dari Accounting 10 tahun lalu, join PT Distributor Sound System 5 tahun lalu sebagai Junior Accountant. Naik jadi Finance Admin 2 tahun lalu ketika company expand ke 3 warehouses. Dia responsible untuk:

- Daily accounting (journal entries, AP/AR)
- Monthly close (trial balance, P&L, balance sheet)
- Financial reports untuk owner & tax office
- Reconciliation across warehouses
- Vendor payment & customer collection

Company pakai Accurate Online untuk accounting, tapi attendance data dari ZKTeco machine harus manual pull ke Excel, surat jalan manual entry, dan inventory data sering out of sync. Sari spend 60% waktunya untuk manual data entry dan reconciliation, bukan analysis.

## Current Situation

**Pain Points:**
1. **Manual Entry Hell:** 60% waktu untuk compile data dari Accurate, Excel, attendance machine
2. **Monthly Close Marathon:** 3 hari untuk reconcile data, find discrepancies, generate reports
3. **Tool Sprawl:** Accurate (accounting) + Excel (attendance) + Manual surat jalan + WhatsApp (approvals)
4. **Subscription Fatigue:** Accurate Rp 300k/bulan, terus naik, no end in sight
5. **No Real-Time Visibility:** Inventory data lag, harus call warehouse untuk check stock

**Current Workflow:**
- Morning: Pull attendance data dari ZKTeco → manual entry ke Excel → calculate lembur/cuti
- Afternoon: Entry surat jalan dari warehouse → reconcile dengan Accurate inventory
- Evening: Entry vendor invoices → match dengan PO → prepare payment list
- Month-end: 3 hari marathon reconciliation → generate reports → submit to owner

**Frustration:** "Saya jadi data entry clerk, bukan analyst. Padahal saya bisa kasih insights kalau punya waktu."

## 6 Driving Forces

### ✅ WANTS (Positive Drivers)

**1. Want: Eliminate Manual Entry**
- **AkuBook Promise:** 95%+ transactions auto-post to journal — Sales Order → Invoice → Journal Entry automatic
- **How:** Cross-module integration — SO/PO/Surat Jalan auto-generate journal entries, no manual intervention
- **Impact:** 60% time freed up untuk strategic analysis

**2. Want: Faster Monthly Close**
- **AkuBook Promise:** Monthly close < 8 hours (vs 3 hari) — real-time reconciliation, auto-generated reports
- **How:** Real-time data sync across modules, pre-configured report templates, one-click month-end close
- **Impact:** No more marathon weekends, work-life balance restored

**3. Want: Single Source of Truth**
- **AkuBook Promise:** All data in one platform — accounting, attendance, inventory, HRM unified
- **How:** Modular architecture dengan shared data layer — no more Excel reconciliation, no more tool sprawl
- **Impact:** Confidence in data accuracy, no more "which version is correct?"

### ❌ FEARS (Negative Drivers)

**4. Fear: Migration Nightmare**
- **AkuBook Answer:** Seamless data import dari Accurate/Excel — opening balances, historical transactions, chart of accounts preserved
- **How:** Built-in migration wizard, step-by-step guidance, validation checks, rollback option
- **Impact:** No "start from zero" pain, audit trail preserved, business continuity maintained

**5. Fear: Learning Curve**
- **AkuBook Answer:** Familiar Accurate-like interface, comprehensive training, 24/7 support
- **How:** UI/UX designed untuk Accurate users, video tutorials, live chat support, community forum
- **Impact:** Productive from Day 1, no productivity dip during transition

**6. Fear: Hidden Costs**
- **AkuBook Answer:** One-time payment Rp 25 juta (vs Accurate Rp 300k/bulan = Rp 18 juta over 5 years) — no surprise fees, no "pay more for more users"
- **How:** Transparent pricing, all modules included, unlimited users, free updates for 1 year
- **Impact:** Predictable budgeting, no subscription fatigue, CFO approval easier
