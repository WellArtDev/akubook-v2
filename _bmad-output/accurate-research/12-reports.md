# 12. Reports (Laporan)

## Overview

Reports module in Accurate Online provides **200+ financial and operational reports** for comprehensive business intelligence and decision-making. All transaction data automatically integrates into reports, providing real-time insights into business performance.

**Key Features:**
- 200+ customizable report types
- Real-time data integration
- AI-powered analysis (Ailita)
- Export to PDF, Excel, CSV
- Report modification and memorization
- Scheduled reports and email delivery
- Dashboard integration

**Access:** Menu Laporan → Daftar Laporan

---

## Report Categories

### 1. Financial Statements (Laporan Keuangan)

**Core Reports:**
- **Neraca (Balance Sheet)** - Assets, liabilities, equity with period comparison
- **Laba/Rugi (Income Statement)** - Revenue, expenses, net profit/loss
- **Arus Kas (Cash Flow)** - Operating, investing, financing activities
- **Perubahan Modal (Equity Changes)** - Capital movements and retained earnings
- **Grafik Harta Bersih** - Net worth visualization over time

**Features:**
- Laba Setelah Pajak (profit after tax) display
- Multi-period comparison
- Customizable format (Indonesian compliance)
- Drill-down to transaction details
- Export to PDF/Excel

**Indonesian Compliance:**
- Format sesuai standar akuntansi Indonesia
- Laba Tahun Ini calculation
- Periode akuntansi support

---

### 2. General Ledger Reports (Laporan Buku Besar)

**Reports:**
- **Rincian Buku Besar** - Detailed ledger transactions by account
- **Neraca Saldo** - Trial balance
- **Jurnal Umum** - General journal entries
- **Buku Besar** - Account summaries

**Customization:**
- Filter by account, date range, branch
- Drill-down to source transactions
- Custom column scripts
- Sorting and grouping options

---

### 3. Accounts Receivable (Laporan Piutang)

**Reports:**
- **Umur Piutang Pelanggan** - Aging analysis (0-30, 31-60, 61-90, >90 days)
- **Faktur Belum Lunas** - Outstanding invoices
- **Pernyataan Piutang** - Customer statement with additions/reductions
- **Rata-rata Pembayaran Pelanggan** - Average payment days
- **Piutang per Departemen** - Receivables by department
- **Piutang Karyawan** - Employee loans and advances

**Parameters:**
- Basis: Tanggal Faktur or Jatuh Tempo
- Age brackets: customizable (default 30 days)
- Customer/employee filtering
- Branch and project filtering

---

### 4. Accounts Payable (Laporan Utang)

**Reports:**
- **Umur Utang Pemasok** - Vendor aging analysis
- **Faktur Pembelian Belum Lunas** - Outstanding purchase invoices
- **Pernyataan Utang** - Vendor statement
- **Rata-rata Pembayaran Pemasok** - Average payment days
- **Rincian Pembayaran Pembelian** - Payment details with day ranges

**Features:**
- Aging calculation: by invoice date or due date
- Vendor performance tracking
- Payment scheduling support

---

### 5. Inventory Reports (Laporan Persediaan)

**Reports:**
- **Nilai Persediaan** - Inventory valuation by item/category
- **Kuantitas Barang per Gudang** - Stock quantity by warehouse
- **Umur Persediaan** - Inventory aging (default 30 days, customizable)
- **Penyesuaian Stok Barang** - Stock adjustments with quantities
- **Kartu Stok** - Stock card with movements
- **Stok Opname** - Physical count vs system comparison

**Analysis:**
- Per kategori barang (by item category)
- Multi-warehouse support
- Conversion unit display
- Negative stock impact on financial statements
- Real-time stock updates

---

### 6. Sales Reports (Laporan Penjualan)

**Reports:**
- **Penjualan Per Barang** - Sales by item
- **Penjualan Per Kategori Barang** - Sales by category
- **Rincian Penjualan per Barang** - Detailed sales with BPP and profit
- **Daftar Faktur Penjualan** - Sales invoice list with transaction count
- **Margin Laba Kotor per Barang** - Gross profit margin by item
- **Harga Nett** - Net price after discounts

**Customization:**
- Period analysis: daily, monthly, yearly
- Category grouping
- BPP (Cost of Goods Sold) and profit display
- Discount and net price calculations
- Custom scripts for additional metrics

---

### 7. Purchase Reports (Laporan Pembelian)

**Reports:**
- **Pembelian Per Barang** - Purchases by item
- **Pembelian Per Pemasok** - Purchases by vendor
- **Daftar Faktur Pembelian** - Purchase invoice list
- **Rincian Pembayaran Pembelian** - Payment details with day ranges
- **Pesanan Pembelian** - Purchase orders (open/closed)
- **Kuantitas Belum Terproses** - Unprocessed quantities

**Features:**
- Vendor analysis
- Purchase order tracking
- Closed order reporting
- Payment term analysis

---

### 8. Tax Reports (Laporan Pajak)

**Reports:**
- **SPT Masa PPN/PPN BM** - VAT return
- **Formulir 1721** - Annual income tax certificate
- **Formulir 1721 A1** - Employee tax form
- **SPT PPh Pasal 21** - Income tax return
- **Bukti Potong PPh Pasal 21** - Tax withholding certificate
- **ePPh 2326 dan ePPh 1771** - Electronic tax forms
- **SPT PPh Pasal 23** - Withholding tax return
- **SPT PPh Pasal 15** - Specific income tax return
- **PPh 22** - Import/purchase tax

**Compliance:**
- Indonesian tax regulation compliance
- Automatic tax calculation
- Integration with Smartlink Tax
- E-filing support
- Tarif PPN 12% support (2026)

---

### 9. Custom Reports (Laporan Kustom)

**Features:**
- **Pusat Laba & Biaya** - Profit/cost center by financial category
- **Kategori Keuangan** - Custom financial categorization (by city, vehicle, project)
- **Modifikasi Laporan** - Report modification with custom scripts
- **Memorize Laporan** - Save customized report formats
- **Kolom Skrip Khusus** - Custom column scripts for calculations

**Customization Options:**
- Add/remove columns
- Custom formulas and calculations
- Grouping and sorting
- Filtering and parameters
- Signature columns
- Date format customization
- Row numbering

## Report Customization

### Modification Features

**Kolom Data (Data Columns):**
- Add/remove fields
- Double-click to add to "Kolom Digunakan"
- Drag to reorder

**Analisa (Analysis):**
- Group by dimensions (category, department, project)
- Add subtotals and grand totals
- Custom row analysis

**Penyaringan (Filtering):**
- Filter by column, operator, value
- Multiple filter conditions
- Date range filtering

**Skrip Khusus (Custom Scripts):**
- Custom formulas (e.g., margin calculation)
- Date calculations (jatuh tempo, umur hari)
- Conditional formatting
- Conversion formulas

### Memorize Reports

**Save Custom Formats:**
1. Modify report layout
2. Click "Memorize" icon
3. Name the custom format
4. Reuse across periods

**Benefits:**
- Consistency across reporting periods
- Time savings
- Standardized formats for team

## AI-Powered Analysis (Ailita)

**Ailita** is Accurate Online's AI accounting assistant for interactive report analysis.

**How to Use:**
1. Open any report (e.g., Laba/Rugi)
2. Click AI logo at top of report
3. Ask questions in natural language
4. Get instant insights and analysis

**Capabilities:**
- Trend analysis
- Anomaly detection
- Performance insights
- Comparative analysis
- Natural language queries

## Best Practices

### Report Access
1. Navigate to **Laporan → Daftar Laporan**
2. Select category or search by name
3. Set parameters (date range, branch, filters)
4. Click **Tampilkan** to generate

### Customization Workflow
1. Generate base report
2. Click **Modifikasi** icon
3. Adjust columns, analysis, filters
4. Test and verify
5. **Memorize** for reuse

### Performance Tips
- Use date range filters to limit data
- Memorize frequently used formats
- Export large reports to Excel for analysis
- Use Ailita for quick insights

### Compliance
- Review tax reports monthly
- Verify aging calculations (basis: invoice date vs due date)
- Check inventory valuation method
- Ensure financial statements match Indonesian standards
