# 1. Dashboard

## Overview

Dashboard module in Accurate Online provides customizable widgets and real-time KPIs for business monitoring. Displays key metrics from all modules in a single view for quick decision-making.

**Priority**: HIGH (user experience)  
**Complexity**: Medium - widget configuration, real-time data, permissions

---

## Features (10 Widgets)

### 1. Financial Summary Widget
**Function**: Display key financial metrics  
**Metrics**:
- Total Revenue (current period)
- Total Expenses
- Net Profit/Loss
- Profit Margin %

### 2. Cash Flow Widget
**Function**: Monitor cash position  
**Metrics**:
- Cash Balance (all accounts)
- Cash In (receipts)
- Cash Out (payments)
- Net Cash Flow

### 3. Receivables Widget
**Function**: Track customer payments  
**Metrics**:
- Total AR Outstanding
- Overdue Amount
- Current vs Overdue breakdown
- Top 5 overdue customers

### 4. Payables Widget
**Function**: Monitor vendor payments  
**Metrics**:
- Total AP Outstanding
- Due This Week
- Overdue Amount
- Payment schedule

### 5. Inventory Widget
**Function**: Stock level monitoring  
**Metrics**:
- Total Inventory Value
- Low Stock Items
- Out of Stock Items
- Fast Moving Items

### 6. Sales Widget
**Function**: Sales performance tracking  
**Metrics**:
- Sales Today/This Week/This Month
- Sales vs Target
- Top Selling Items
- Sales by Category

### 7. Purchase Widget
**Function**: Procurement monitoring  
**Metrics**:
- Purchase This Month
- Open POs
- Pending Receipts
- Top Vendors

### 8. Profit & Loss Widget
**Function**: P&L summary  
**Metrics**:
- Revenue
- COGS
- Gross Profit
- Operating Expenses
- Net Profit

### 9. Balance Sheet Widget
**Function**: Financial position snapshot  
**Metrics**:
- Total Assets
- Total Liabilities
- Total Equity
- Current Ratio

### 10. Custom Metrics Widget
**Function**: User-defined KPIs  
**Capabilities**:
- Custom formulas
- Multi-metric display
- Trend charts
- Drill-down to details

---

## Customization Options

### Widget Configuration
- Add/remove widgets
- Resize and reposition
- Set refresh intervals
- Configure data filters

### User Preferences
- Save custom layouts
- Role-based dashboards
- Branch-specific views
- Period selection

### Real-Time Updates
- Auto-refresh intervals (1min, 5min, 15min, manual)
- Live data from all modules
- Notification alerts

---

## Permissions

### Role-Based Access
- Admin: Full dashboard access
- Manager: Department-specific widgets
- Operator: Limited view (own transactions)

### Data Filtering
- Branch-level filtering
- Department filtering
- Date range selection

---

## Integration Points

**Data Sources**:
- General Ledger (financial metrics)
- Sales (revenue, AR)
- Purchasing (expenses, AP)
- Inventory (stock levels)
- Cash & Bank (cash flow)

**Drill-Down**:
- Click widget → detailed report
- Interactive charts
- Export to Excel/PDF
