# Accurate Insight - Business Intelligence & Analytics

## Overview

**Accurate Insight** = Ailita AI + 200+ Reports. Advanced analytics module combining AI-powered financial analysis with comprehensive reporting for data-driven decision making.

**Core Components:**
- **Ailita AI**: 24/7 virtual assistant for financial analysis
- **200+ Reports**: Comprehensive financial, operational, and analytical reports
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: Live KPI monitoring and visualization
- **Predictive Analytics**: AI-driven forecasting and trend analysis

---

## 1. Ailita AI - Virtual Financial Analyst

### 1.1 Core Capabilities

**Analisis Tren & Perbandingan Periode**
- Compare financial performance across periods (month-over-month, year-over-year)
- Track revenue growth, cost changes, profitability trends
- Natural language insights: "Revenue bulan depan diprediksi naik 15%"

**Deteksi Anomali Keuangan**
- Automatic detection of unusual transactions
- Identify sudden expense spikes or revenue drops
- Branch-specific anomaly alerts
- Fraud pattern recognition (future roadmap)

**Kalkulasi & Interpretasi Rasio Keuangan**
- Auto-calculate profitability, liquidity, solvency ratios
- Plain-language explanations of financial health
- Context-aware recommendations

**Analisis Per Cabang/Unit Bisnis**
- Multi-branch performance comparison
- Identify top/bottom performers
- Unit-specific insights and recommendations

**Penyajian Insight Sederhana**
- Translate complex accounting terms to everyday language
- Visual summaries with key takeaways
- Actionable recommendations

### 1.2 How to Use Ailita

\\\
1. Login → Pilih Data Usaha → Klik Laporan Keuangan
2. Pilih laporan (e.g., Laba Rugi, Neraca, Arus Kas)
3. Klik logo AI di panel atas
4. Ajukan pertanyaan atau minta analisis
5. Ailita memberikan insight real-time
\\\

**Sample Questions:**
- "Bagaimana performa penjualan bulan ini vs bulan lalu?"
- "Apa produk terlaris di kuartal ini?"
- "Cabang mana yang paling profitable?"
- "Bagaimana meningkatkan cash flow?"

**Current (2026):**

- Natural language Q&A

**Future:**
