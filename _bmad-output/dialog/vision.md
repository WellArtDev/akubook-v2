# Vision Statement: AkuBook

**Date:** 2026-05-12

---

## Vision Statement

AkuBook adalah solusi bisnis all-in-one yang membebaskan bisnis Indonesia dari subscription fatigue dan vendor lock-in. Dengan satu kali pembayaran, bisnis mendapatkan kontrol penuh atas data mereka melalui deployment on-premise, sambil mengelola accounting, attendance, dan HRM dalam satu dashboard terpadu. Dari distributor hingga retail dan jasa, AkuBook menghilangkan kebutuhan akan multiple tools terpisah dan biaya bulanan yang terus membengkak. Integrasi dengan hardware existing (ZKTeco) dan roadmap mobile app memastikan AkuBook bisa adopt secara bertahap tanpa buang investasi yang sudah ada.

---

## Core Elements

### Freedom
- One-time payment model (no subscription)
- On-premise deployment option
- Full data control and ownership

### Simplicity
- Single unified dashboard
- Eliminates multiple disconnected tools
- All business operations in one place

### Universality
- Horizontal solution for any business type
- Distributor, retail, jasa, and more
- Not industry-specific

### Completeness
- **Accounting:** Full accounting like Accurate Online
- **Attendance:** Geo-location (map), face recognition, ZKTeco integration
- **HRM:** Payroll, leave management, overtime (lembur), cuti

### Pragmatic
- Integrates with existing hardware investments (ZKTeco)
- Mobile app roadmap for on-the-go access
- Phased adoption without throwing away existing tools

---

## Problem Being Solved

**Current pain points:**
1. Multiple separate tools (accounting, attendance, HRM)
2. Subscription fatigue — monthly fees that keep growing
3. No on-premise option — forced cloud dependency
4. Vendor lock-in and data control issues

**Target outcome:**
- One dashboard for all business operations
- Multi-role access (Owner, Admin Finance, Sales, Purchasing)
- On-premise control with data sovereignty
- One-time payment eliminates recurring costs

---

## Target Market

- **Geography:** Indonesia
- **Business types:** All (distributor, retail, jasa, etc.)
- **Size:** SME/UKM primarily, scalable to larger operations
- **Pain point:** Businesses tired of subscription models and wanting data control
