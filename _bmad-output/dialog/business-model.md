# Business Model: AkuBook

**Date:** 2026-05-12

---

## Business Model

**Type:** B2B (Business-to-Business)
**Target:** Small & mid-size companies (10-100+ employees)

---

## Buyer vs User

### Buyer
- **Who:** Company owner / decision maker
- **Decision process:** Direct evaluation and purchase
- **Pain point:** Subscription fatigue, tool sprawl, data control

### Users
- **Who:** Multi-role users within the company
  - Owner (dashboard, reports, oversight)
  - Finance Admin (accounting, payroll)
  - Sales (sales orders, customer management)
  - Purchasing (purchase orders, supplier management)
  - Staff (attendance, leave requests)
- **Access:** Unlimited users per company license

---

## Sales & Discovery

### Current Strategy
- **Channel:** Word-of-mouth, direct outreach
- **Sales cycle:** Direct to owner (fast decision if pain resonates)
- **Demo approach:** Show industry-specific setup to prove flexibility

### Future Channels
- Website with self-service demo
- Partner network (accounting firms, consultants)
- Marketplace presence (if applicable)

## Pricing Structure

### Base License
- **Model:** One-time payment per company
- **Includes:** All modules (accounting, attendance, HRM, retail, distributor, manufacturing, etc.)
- **Users:** Unlimited users per company
- **Multi-company:** Unlimited companies per license

### Modular System
- **All modules included in base** — no separate module purchases
- **Auto-configuration:** Industry selection at setup → auto-enable relevant modules
  - Retail → POS, inventory, basic accounting
  - Distributor → Multi-warehouse, purchase orders, supplier management
  - Manufacturing → Production planning, BOM, work orders
  - Service → Project management, time tracking, invoicing
- **Flexible:** Users can enable/disable any module as needs change
- **No artificial limits:** All features accessible, company decides what to use

### Add-Ons (Paid)
- **Custom features:** Bespoke development for specific needs
- **Custom integrations:** Connect to proprietary systems
- **Industry-specific customizations:** Unique workflows or reports

### Support Model
- **Included:** 1 month post-purchase support
- **Updates/bug fixes:** Included for lifetime (part of ownership)
- **Paid support options:**
  - **Per-incident:** Pay as needed for specific issues
  - **Monthly retainer:** Ongoing support contract (company chooses)
- **Priority support:** Faster response times, dedicated contact

---

## Licensing Details

### Per Company License
- **Scope:** One license covers one legal entity
- **Users:** Unlimited users within that company
- **Multi-company:** One license can manage multiple companies (holding companies, accounting firms)
- **Deployment:** On-premise or cloud (company chooses)

### No Per-User Fees
- **Rationale:** Simplifies pricing, removes growth penalty
- **Competitive advantage:** Competitors charge per user (cost scales with growth)
- **Customer benefit:** Predictable costs, no fear of adding users

---

## Business Model Rationale

### Why This Model Works

**Market Fit:**
- SMEs hate subscription models (predictable pain point)
- One-time payment removes ongoing cost anxiety
- All-features-included eliminates decision paralysis ("which tier do I need?")

**Competitive Positioning:**
- Accurate, Jurnal, Mekari: Monthly per-user fees
- AkuBook: One-time per-company, unlimited users
- Clear differentiation in pricing conversation

**Revenue Strategy:**
- **Initial sale:** Base license (primary revenue)
- **Recurring:** Support contracts (optional, predictable)
- **Project-based:** Custom development (high-margin)

**Customer Success:**
- No artificial limits → customers use what they need
- Modular flexibility → adapt as business changes
- Multi-company support → serve accounting firms, holding companies

## Product Strategy Implications

### Must-Have Capabilities

**Modular Architecture:**
- Rock-solid enable/disable without breaking dependencies
- Clean module boundaries and interfaces
- Graceful degradation if module disabled

**Onboarding Flow:**
- Industry selection → auto-configuration
- Guided setup per enabled module
- Easy module activation later (no re-setup)

**Documentation:**
- Per-module documentation (users only learn what they need)
- Industry-specific quick-start guides
- Video tutorials per module

**Multi-Company Management:**
- Switch between companies seamlessly
- Consolidated reporting across companies
- Separate data isolation per company

### Sales Enablement

**Demo Strategy:**
- Show industry-specific setup (retail vs distributor)
- Demonstrate module flexibility (enable/disable live)
- Compare pricing vs competitors (one-time vs monthly)

**Positioning:**
- "All features included" vs tiered pricing
- "Unlimited users" vs per-user fees
- "Own your software" vs subscription lock-in

**Upsell Opportunities:**
- Support contracts (monthly retainer)
- Custom integrations (project-based)
- Training and onboarding services
- Priority support upgrades

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Concerns

**"One-time payment = no updates?"**
- **Answer:** Lifetime updates and bug fixes included
- **Proof:** Clear update policy in license agreement

**"What if I outgrow it?"**
- **Answer:** Modular system scales with business
- **Proof:** Multi-company support, unlimited users

**"What about support after 1 month?"**
- **Answer:** Flexible support options (per-incident or monthly)
- **Proof:** Transparent support pricing, no forced contracts

**"Can I migrate my data?"**
- **Answer:** Import from Accurate, Jurnal, Excel
- **Proof:** Migration tools and documentation
